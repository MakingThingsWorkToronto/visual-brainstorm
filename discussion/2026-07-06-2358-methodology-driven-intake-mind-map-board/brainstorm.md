Round 1 — diverge. Six philosophies for how the New Discussion panel drives a session into a METHODOLOGY, using the new mind-map board as the test case. ### Options presented
- **Method Gallery** (`gallery`) — Named methodology cards at intake; the brief form adapts to the chosen method and the session opens already in its shape. {explicit, cards}
- **Concierge Interview** (`concierge`) — You write the brief; two adaptive questions later the panel recommends a methodology with reasons — accept or override. {guided, wizard}
- **Seed Shape Inference** (`seed-inference`) — No setup: sketch or type anything, the system infers methodology from the seed's shape and announces it — one-tap override. {inferred, zero-friction}
- **The Structure Dial** (`dial`) — No named methods at all — a single explore↔build dial maps to the phase arc; middle positions blend option grids with living structure. {abstract, dial}
- **The Brief IS a Mind Map** (`canvas-first`) — Wild card: the New Discussion panel IS a live mind-elixir canvas — the brief is authored as nodes, Claude grows the same tree, phases become tree operations. {wild, live-tree}
- **Round Zero — Methods as a Board** (`meta-board`) — Wild card: no panel change — round 1 of every session is a board whose OPTIONS are methodologies, chosen through the tool's own grammar. {wild, self-similar}
